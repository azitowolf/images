EF Tech Recruitment Site

{Interactive website and UX}

Company culture

The aim of this project was to create a beautiful site that captured the spirit of Education Firsts software culture - highlighting what makes the UXers, developers, and designers here awesome to work with.

Tight quarters

We began this project with a small team of developers and designers in 2x3 meter sq room full of potato chips and coffee. We worked collaboratively, prototyping {website concepts} and design mockups simultaneously

  • The Site

People and problems

We decided that what makes us love our jobs at EF are the problems that we get to solve, and the people that we get to solve them with. The site opens with a slider that asks the viewer to think about some of the cool problems they might see working here.

  • The Site

Cool tech

The best software frameworks are powerful and structural, but allow you to think creatively and improvise. I got to work with reactjs for the duration of the project and made a plugin based on it.

  • Check it out

Design and develop

Overall the project allowed me to flex my creative muscle and think as a UXer and a developer to build a beautiful and sleek site - also one that highlights the beautiful people I work with :)
